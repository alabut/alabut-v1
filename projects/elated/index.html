Elated Salesforce app

Hello Edwin and Agni! Here's the proposed designs for the registration screen - feel free to IM, email or call me with feedback.

Requirements:

  1. highlight the required fields and consolidate the pre-filled ones
  2. clearly explain that the registration is one-time only
  3. create a professional-looking branded color scheme that will carry throughout the web app and can be extended to a standalone website

Introduction

One thing that I did that needs pointing out is that I made up a fictional name for the app as a placeholder for the logo. I came up with "Cloudalize" as a reference to storing data in the cloud. This is something easy to switch out when you guys come up with the final product name. You're welcome to steal Cloudalize if you like it - the domain is available and I won't charge extra for it :)

Wireframe Design

Before going over the version with a branded color scheme, let's take a look at just the layout and grouping of info in a gray wireframe:

Some things to notice about the different UI elements:

  1. There's a welcome message explaining that we need more info to create the account.
  2. The pre-filled information is presented w/o form fields so that they can skip over the step if the info is correct.
  3. The space to the right has room for notes, like the one that let's the user know this is something they only need to fill out once.
  4. The terms and conditions are linked to right before the submit button, rather than a standalone step.

Color Design With Branding

This is the same basic layout, but with a white-on-green color scheme and with a transparent gradient box to group together the form elements.

And that's it for now - take a look and let me know what you guys think. If you approve, then we can busy building this and making the error states and other edge cases as well. Thanks!

12/11/08 - Update #4 - File Viewer Widget

Notes:

  • The use of icons will help make the different possible actions a little easier to understand and less cluttered - view, delete and add.
